Fresh Strawberry

Here are the ingredients:

  • 1 pint fresh strawberries, hulled
  • ¼ cup white sugar, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • ½ teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Here are the steps:

  1. Combine strawberries, sugar, 2 tablespoons water, and balsamic vinegar in a saucepan; bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Whisk 2 tablespoons water and cornstarch together in a small bowl.
  4. Whisk cornstarch mixture into strawberry mixture. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from heat.
  5. Transfer mixture to a blender and puree until smooth.
